# Knowledge Base: Business Plans & Services

- Cost: $13.50/month

- Minutes: Unlimited to all networks

## TECHNICAL SUPPORT

### Support Levels
- Basic (all plans): 9:00 AM – 9:00 PM, 4-hour response time
- Priority ("Team" plan): 24/7, 1-hour response time
- Dedicated Manager: Available for 50+ lines

### Common Technical Issues

#### No Internet Connection
1.  Check your balance.
2.  Restart the device.
3.  Verify APN settings: `internet.carrier.com`
4.  Call support: 8-800-XXX-XX-XX

#### Outgoing/Incoming Calls Failing
1.  Check signal strength (at least 2 bars required).
2.  Ensure Airplane Mode is off.
3.  Check call forwarding status: `*#21#`
4.  Contact support with the specific error code.

#### Virtual PBX Issues
1.  Log in to the client portal: `lk.carrier.com`
2.  Verify routing settings.
3.  Ensure employee numbers are added correctly.
4.  PBX Tech Support: `pbx-support@carrier.com`

## BILLING & PAYMENTS

### Payment Methods
- Bank transfer (via invoice)
- Auto-pay via credit/debit card
- Client portal (online payment)
- Cash at service centers

### Invoices & Reports
- Invoices are issued on the 1st of every month.
- Call logs: Available in the portal for the last 3 months.
- VAT invoices/Completion certificates: Upon request or automatically via Electronic Document Interchange (EDI).

### Arrears & Suspension
- Warning: Sent 5 days before suspension.
- Blocking: Occurs if debt is older than 30 days.
- Restoration: Within 2 hours after payment is processed.
